Pitch Day Schedule 
The event will run from 9:00 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. Lunch will be provided while judges deliberate. Please plan to arrive no later than 8:45 a.m. 

 

Presentations 
Each team will have 8 minutes to pitch, followed by a Q&A with the judges. 
All presentation materials are due by 4:00 p.m. on Tuesday, March 17. 

Please submit your presentation as a PowerPoint file and embed all videos, demos, or other media directly in the deck. Wi-Fi at City Hall can be unreliable, so this will help ensure your presentation runs smoothly when we download it in advance. 

 

Practice Rounds (Optional) 
The City Hall Council Chambers will be available on the afternoon of Monday, March 16 for sound checks and practice runs. Judges will be available to provide feedback on your presentation, public speaking and ideas in general. This is completely optional, but a great opportunity to test your setup and get comfortable with space and technology. If you’re interested, please indicate this in the form below and we’ll follow up with details.
